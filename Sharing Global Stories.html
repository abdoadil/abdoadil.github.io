Sharing Global Stories at Chandigarh University 🌍🎙️

I am thrilled to have attended the official launch of "Beyond The Border," a new program by Radio Punjab, the community radio of Chandigarh University.

As someone who has spent the last few years navigating the challenges and triumphs of life away from home, this initiative feels incredibly personal.

The show is dedicated to highlighting the unique journeys of international students capturing how we embrace new cultures, overcome language barriers, and find a second home here in Punjab.

It was inspiring to be surrounded by fellow students from all corners of the globe, each with a story of resilience and ambition.
